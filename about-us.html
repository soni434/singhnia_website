About Us

The Singhania Group commenced the business at a modest scale in 1975, with the trading of Industrial Chemicals like - Palm Fatty Acid, Calcium Carbide & Ferro Alloys at Ludhiana, a reknowned Industrial city of Punjab. Over the time, the group gradually diversified and introduced a whole new range of products such as Ores & Minerals, Steel & Non-Ferrous Metals, Wire Rods, Ferrous & Non-Ferrous Scrap, HR & CR Coils and Industrial Chemicals. In a very short span of time, the group captured the market in North India and hope to continue in the years to come. Our work ethics and dedication along with the trust and satisfaction of our customers is leading us on the path of expansion. Singhania International Limited was established in the year 1995 and caters to the requirement of wide spectrum of industries. Today, Singhania International Limited is primarily engaged in the business of manufacturing Carbon, Alloy and Stainless-Steel Wires. The company has not limited its scope to Steel Wires but also procures and supplies huge quantities of Ferro Alloys, Steel Rounds, Fasteners, Ores & Minerals, Chemicals like Boric Acid & Borax, Foundry Supplies & Non-Ferrous Metals. Over the years, the company has gained immense reputation for its unmatched expertise in manufacturing Steel Wires, Cold Forged Components and Fasteners

Headquartered at Ludhiana (Punjab) 300 kms from the National Capital New Delhi, the company's manufacturing unit is spread across an area of 120,000 Sq ft. In addition, there is an indigenous packing and storage space of over 50,000 Sq ft for handling incoming raw materials and finished goods at Sahnewal a very nearby city.

We at SIL firmly believe in working hand in hand with our customers. Our scope of business is not limited by any boundaries. Today, we both procure and supply the goods in the global markets.
