Meet our Management

Anil Kumar Singhania

Managing Director

Anil Kumar Singhania is a visionary entrepreneur and the founder of Singhania International Limited. With a strong educational background in commerce and extensive knowledge of finance, he has been successfully leading the company since four decades. Throughout his career, he has demonstrated exceptional leadership skills, strategic thinking, and the ability to navigate through various challenges.Anil Kumar Singhania's entrepreneurial journey has been marked by a steadfast commitment to his vision of transforming Singhania International Limited into a major global enterprise. He possesses a forward-thinking mindset, constantly exploring innovative ideas and strategies to propel the company forward. By fostering a culture of excellence, he inspires his team to strive for continuous improvement and push the boundaries of success.Under Anil Kumar Singhania's guidance, Singhania International Limited has achieved significant milestones and established a strong market presence. He has been instrumental in building strategic partnerships, expanding the company's product portfolio, and exploring new avenues for growth. His ability to identify emerging trends and capitalize on market opportunities has enabled the company to stay ahead of the competition.Beyond his role as Managing Director, Anil Kumar Singhania is known for his philanthropic contribution and commitment to corporate social responsibility. He believes in giving back to society and actively supports initiatives related to education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability. Through his leadership, Singhania International Limited has embraced sustainable practices, making a positive impact on both the business and the community

Anshul Singhania

Executive Director

Anshul Singhania is an ambitious and self-driven individual, holding the position of Director at Singhania International Limited, a leading fastener company. With an Engineering degree from Dubai BITS Pilani and a Master's degree from the prestigious London School of Economics, he brings a unique blend of technical expertise and business acumen to his role. His ability to think beyond the conventional boundaries and strive for greatness has set him apart as a visionary leader.With relentless work ethics and dedication he exemplifies the value of hard work and is willing to go extra mile to accomplish extra ordinary feats. His commitment to the company's vision is evident in his hands-on approach, as he is actively involved in all aspects of the business, from strategy development to operational execution. His knack to identify emerging trends and adapt quickly to market demands has ensured the company to stay ahead of competition. By leveraging his engineering background, he brings a unique perspective to the fastener industry, implementing innovative solutions and driving continuous improvement.A very gentle and pragmatic person, who very well recognizes the importance of giving back to society hence actively supports initiatives related to education, social welfare, and community development. As Director, he remains focused on driving innovation, expanding market reach, and solidifying the company's position as a leader in the fastener industry. With his persistent hard work, visionary thinking and commitment to excellence, Anshual Singhania continues to lead Singhania International Limited towards new horizons.
